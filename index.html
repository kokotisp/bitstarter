Radio-Pi is a portable web-radio player with 2.8 inch TFT, touchscreen and wifi, powered by a raspberry pi computer. It can also act as a media player and comes with 4gb storage.
Listen to your favorite station with Radio-Pi.

Radio-Pi specs:
- Raspberry-pi model A as the heart of the system.
- 2.8 TFT with resistive touchscreen.
- 5 watt sound output
- Wifi enabled
- 4gb storage for audio/video files
- HDMI out/Audio out
- Large web radio database
